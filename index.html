How we work

Search built on
qualification,
not volume

We decline briefs that are not a good fit. That discipline — applied from the first conversation — is what makes each search we do run more predictably and produce better outcomes.

01

Qualify the brief properly

We assess niche fit, salary alignment, decision-maker access, urgency and commercial return before accepting any search. Poor-fit briefs get declined or renegotiated — not worked at low intensity while the best candidates get away.

02

Build a targeted market map

We identify the specific companies, backgrounds and candidate profiles that match your requirement — not a database trawl. Every search starts with a defined strategy, not a keyword search.

03

Engage with genuine market expertise

Candidates engage because the conversation is credible — informed by real understanding of the sector, the product and the career opportunity, not a scripted pitch.

04

Deliver a precise shortlist

Our shortlists include clear rationale, candidate motivations, compensation data and interview readiness assessment. Not a stack of CVs with covering email.

05

Manage through to placement

We stay close through interviews, offers and start dates. Most offer-stage fallouts are preventable — we identify the risks and surface them early.

Why we exist

The generalist problem in fintech hiring

Most recruiters treat fintech as a vertical bolt-on to a generalist practice. They know enough to write a job description, but not enough to evaluate whether a candidate truly understands the market, the buyer or the product.

OakFIN was built to close that gap. We recruit exclusively in financial technology and specialist software — which means our domain knowledge is genuine, not borrowed from a job spec.

Our approach

A manageable search load — by design

We keep our active search load manageable. That means more time spent on each brief: proper qualification, thorough market mapping, and candidate conversations that go well beyond a CV summary.

We use AI to accelerate the research and operational side of search. The commercial judgement stays human.

Global reach

London-anchored, globally capable

OakFIN's primary market is the UK with London as the anchor. We have active search capability across Europe and North America, and global experience for niche or senior mandates wherever the role demands it.

London UK Western Europe New York North America APAC (select)

Our commitment

Honest about what we take on

We decline briefs that aren't a good fit — roles outside our niche, with unrealistic salary expectations, or without the process quality that gives a search a reasonable chance of success. That discipline makes us considerably more useful to the clients we do take on.
